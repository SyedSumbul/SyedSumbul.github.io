Selected Projects

Plant-based Food Choices on University Campuses

A behaviour change proposal focused on increasing plant-based food choices among students and staff using value-based decision-making.

Message Framing to Reduce Meat Consumption

A research proposal examining environmental and animal welfare framing in behavioural intentions to reduce meat consumption.

Sense of Agency in Schizophrenia

A critical essay exploring predictive and postdictive accounts of altered sense of agency in schizophrenia, including ethical and philosophical implications.

Depression Beyond the Chemical Imbalance Account

A critical essay examining the monoamine hypothesis of depression, neuroplasticity, treatment response, and personalised approaches to mental health.
